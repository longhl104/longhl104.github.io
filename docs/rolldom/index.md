## Overview

Rolldom is a turn-based strategy board game developed by **LongCode**. Players compete to conquer territories on a map by rolling dice to attack and defend regions — either locally or against others online.

The game is designed for fun and fair play, with a strong focus on user privacy and minimal data collection.

---

## Features

- **Turn-based gameplay**: Roll dice to attack and conquer regions on a shared map
- **Online multiplayer**: Play with friends or random opponents via Unity Relay — no account required
- **Local gameplay**: Supports local pass-and-play on a single device
- **Privacy-first design**: No personal accounts, no personal data stored on our servers
- **All settings stored locally**: Game preferences are saved only on your device

---

## User Data and Permissions

Rolldom requests access to your Google account solely for the purpose of **identifying you as a unique player in online multiplayer sessions**. Specifically:

- Your **Google account email and display name** may be used to create an in-game player profile
- This allows other players in your session to see who they are playing against
- No data is sold, shared with third parties for advertising, or used outside of the game

For full details on what data is collected and how it is used, see our [Privacy Policy]({% link docs/rolldom/privacy-policy.md %}).
