# Privacy Policy for Rolldom

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026  
**Developer:** LongCode  
**App Name:** Rolldom  
**Contact:** longlunglay1998@gmail.com

---

## 1. Overview

Rolldom is a turn-based strategy board game developed by LongCode. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Rolldom is designed to be played with minimal data collection.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
- We do **not** collect precise location data (GPS)
- We do **not** access your camera or microphone
- We do **not** access your contacts or files
- We do **not** collect payment information

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you play Rolldom online multiplayer, the following technical data may be processed temporarily to provide the service:

| Data | Purpose | Stored? |
|---|---|---|
| Device IP address | Required for online multiplayer matchmaking | No — used in transit only |
| Session identifiers | Identify your game session during multiplayer | No — discarded after session ends |
| Game state data (regions, dice, turn) | Synchronise multiplayer game state between players | No — discarded after session ends |

### 2.3 Crash and Diagnostics Data

If the app crashes, anonymised crash reports may be collected by **Unity Analytics** and **Google Play** to help us identify and fix bugs. This data does not identify you personally and includes:

- Device model and Android version
- App version
- Stack trace of the crash

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Rolldom uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Unity Gaming Services** | Multiplayer networking and relay | [unity.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy) |
| **Google Play Services** | App distribution and crash reporting | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| **Google AdMob** | In-game advertisements | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

### Advertising (AdMob)

Rolldom displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve relevant ads:

- **Advertising ID** — a resettable device identifier used for ad personalisation
- **App activity** — in-app interactions used to measure ad performance
- **Approximate location** — derived from IP address for regional ad targeting

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

- On Android: **Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID** (Android 12+) or **Opt out of Ads Personalisation** (Android 11 and below)

We do not sell your personal data. Ad data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy.

---

## 4. Online Multiplayer

When you use the online multiplayer feature, your game session is routed through Unity Relay servers. During this time:

- Your IP address is used to establish the connection
- Game data (moves, dice rolls, region ownership) is transmitted to other players in your session
- No personally identifiable information is required to play
- Session data is not stored after the game ends

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Rolldom does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The game does not require account creation or any personal information to play.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at longlunglay1998@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

Since Rolldom does not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data at risk from our side. Anonymised crash data is handled by Unity and Google according to their respective security standards.

Game settings (such as player count preference and game mode) are stored **locally on your device only** using Android's shared preferences (`PlayerPrefs`). This data never leaves your device.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us at longlunglay1998@gmail.com.

If you are in the European Union (EU/EEA), you have rights under GDPR. If you are in California, you have rights under CCPA. As we do not collect personal data, these rights are satisfied by default.
